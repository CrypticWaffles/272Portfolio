  • Android Sensor Overview
    A reputable and official overview of sensors on android phones and how to use them for Android developers. This site lists all the various sensor types, how to reference them, insert them into code, the data types they use, and various other details about them. There are also links to more individual pages with more info on each type of sensor and how to use them.
  • Rating: 5 out of 5

    A very detailed process of how you can integrate various sensors into your code, product, or app.
